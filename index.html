HENESSY ARTISTRY VS CLASS VIII
Since its first edition, which was won by renowned lyricist “Ice Prince Zamani”, the VS Class platform has served as a stepping stone for gifted rappers like VADER THE WILDCARD, BARRY LANES, LAUDREYES, MATOSAN whilst delivering to Nigerians a display of lyrical ingenuity and prowess.

Now in its 8th season, this year's VS Class auditions will be held in Owerri, Abuja and Lagos to discover the next best talent to hit Nigeria's vibrant Hip hop scene. The Hennessy VS Class live audition offers all budding and talented Rappers an opportunity to showcase their craft to the finest MC's in the game.
